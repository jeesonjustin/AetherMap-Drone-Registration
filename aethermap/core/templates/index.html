How it Works

Three simple steps to fly compliant or get services fast.

1

Create your account

Sign up as a Drone User, Service Provider, or Farmer. Complete your profile & verification.

2

Check zones & submit

Use the map to confirm your area. Drone users file a flight request; farmers submit a service request.

3

Get approval or match

Admins approve flights. Farmers get matched with providers. Track everything on your dashboard.
